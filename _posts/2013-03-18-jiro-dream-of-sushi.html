The lessons I learned from watching this movie.
- Improve your craft everyday because there are always room to make it better.
- Finish what you started as if there is not alternative or safety net.
- Once you choose the career path, enjoy it instead of complaining about it.
- Love what you do and do it very well, that's the way to earn respect from customers and peers.
