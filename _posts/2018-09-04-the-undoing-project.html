After reading the Thinking, Fast and Slow, I picked up this book to discover more about the author written by my favorite author Michael Lewis.

It's amazing to read about the story between Amos Tversky and Daniel Kahneman and their collaboration on their project that has helped me think from another perspective.

It's a good book to read.
