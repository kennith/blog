I learned about this book through the Bill Gates' reading list. Since I am doing the fiction and non-fiction rotation for my reading, I picked up this book for reading pleasure.

The Rosie Project is a page turner. The characters in the book are fun and the pace of the book are quick.

There is not much I want to spill in here because it will ruin the fun for potential readers. However, if you enjoy watching the Big Bang Theory, this book is for you.
